Long Straddle Spread

The long straddle is an options strategy involving the purchase of a call and a put option with the same strike price and expiration date. Key features include:

  • Profit from significant price movement in the underlying asset.
  • Unlimited profit potential due to the call option.
  • Limited risk to the premium paid.

This strategy is used when anticipating a volatile market but uncertain about the direction.
