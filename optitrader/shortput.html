Short Put

The short put, or naked put, is a bullish options strategy where the investor sells a put option with the expectation that the underlying asset's price will rise or remain stable.

Key Points:

  • Sell a put option to generate income.
  • Obligation to buy the underlying asset at the strike price if assigned.
  • Profit limited to the premium received.
  • Used when the investor expects the underlying asset's price to rise or remain stable.
